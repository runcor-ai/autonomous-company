# Welcome Email Sequence Template

## Overview
Automated welcome sequence for new subscribers/customers with personalized onboarding.

## AI Prompt Template
```
Create a 5-email welcome sequence for [BUSINESS_TYPE] targeting [TARGET_AUDIENCE]. 

Email 1 (Immediate): Welcome + Set Expectations
- Thank them for joining
- Explain what they'll receive
- Set expectations for frequency
- Include first value piece

Email 2 (Day 2): Introduce Your Story
- Share origin story
- Build personal connection
- Explain your mission/values
- Include social proof

Email 3 (Day 4): Deliver Core Value
- Provide your best free resource
- Explain how to implement
- Include case study or example
- Encourage engagement

Email 4 (Day 7): Social Proof & Community
- Share customer testimonials
- Highlight community aspects
- Include user-generated content
- Invite to join social channels

Email 5 (Day 10): Soft Pitch & Next Steps
- Present main offer naturally
- Focus on transformation/results
- Include limited-time bonus
- Clear call-to-action

Tone: [FRIENDLY/PROFESSIONAL/CASUAL]
Industry: [SPECIFIC_INDUSTRY]
```

## Customization Variables
- [BUSINESS_TYPE]: Your business category
- [TARGET_AUDIENCE]: Specific demographic
- [FRIENDLY/PROFESSIONAL/CASUAL]: Tone preference
- [SPECIFIC_INDUSTRY]: Your industry vertical

## Implementation Steps
1. Input your variables into the AI prompt
2. Generate the sequence
3. Review and personalize each email
4. Set up automation triggers
5. Test the flow before going live

## Expected Results
- 40-60% open rates
- 15-25% click rates
- 80%+ completion rate through sequence
- 5-10% conversion to paid offers
