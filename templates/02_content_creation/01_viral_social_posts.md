# Viral Social Media Post Generator

## Overview
AI template for creating engaging social media posts with high viral potential across platforms.

## AI Prompt Template
```
Create 10 viral social media posts for [PLATFORM] about [TOPIC/INDUSTRY]. 

Requirements:
- Target audience: [TARGET_DEMOGRAPHIC]
- Brand voice: [BRAND_VOICE]
- Goal: [ENGAGEMENT/AWARENESS/LEADS/SALES]

For each post include:
1. Hook (first line that stops scrolling)
2. Value/insight/entertainment
3. Call-to-action
4. Relevant hashtags (3-5)
5. Engagement prompt (question/poll/request)

Post types to include:
- Educational carousel (3 posts)
- Behind-the-scenes (2 posts)
- User-generated content prompt (2 posts)
- Trending topic commentary (2 posts)
- Value-packed thread starter (1 post)

Format for [TWITTER/LINKEDIN/INSTAGRAM/TIKTOK]
Optimize for [PLATFORM] algorithm and best practices.
```

## Platform Variations
- **Twitter**: 280 chars, trending hashtags, thread potential
- **LinkedIn**: Professional tone, industry insights, thought leadership
- **Instagram**: Visual focus, stories integration, Reels ideas
- **TikTok**: Trendy, entertaining, sound suggestions

## Customization Variables
- [PLATFORM]: Primary social platform
- [TOPIC/INDUSTRY]: Your niche area
- [TARGET_DEMOGRAPHIC]: Age, interests, pain points
- [BRAND_VOICE]: Funny, authoritative, friendly, educational
- [ENGAGEMENT/AWARENESS/LEADS/SALES]: Primary goal

## Viral Elements Framework
1. **Pattern Interrupt**: Unexpected opening
2. **Emotional Trigger**: Joy, surprise, curiosity, anger
3. **Social Currency**: Make people look smart for sharing
4. **Practical Value**: Actionable tips people can use
5. **Storytelling**: Personal anecdotes or case studies

## Implementation Checklist
- [ ] Test hooks with small audience first
- [ ] A/B test posting times
- [ ] Monitor engagement in first hour
- [ ] Engage with early commenters
- [ ] Cross-promote on other platforms
- [ ] Repurpose top performers

## Success Metrics
- 3x normal engagement rate
- 50% increase in followers
- 25% boost in profile visits
- 10+ shares/reposts per viral post
