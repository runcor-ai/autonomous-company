# Sales Playbook: AI Business Automation Suite

## Target Prospects
**Primary**: SMBs with 10-500 employees in these sectors:
- E-commerce and retail
- Professional services 
- SaaS companies
- Manufacturing with customer service needs

**Pain Points to Address**:
- High customer service costs
- Manual sales processes
- Operational inefficiencies
- Scaling challenges

## Sales Process

### 1. Prospecting & Outreach
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator targeting
- Cold email sequences (3-touch sequence)
- Industry conference networking
- Referral program activation

### 2. Discovery Questions
- "How many customer inquiries do you handle monthly?"
- "What's your current customer service cost per ticket?"
- "How much time does your sales team spend on manual tasks?"
- "What's preventing you from scaling operations?"

### 3. Value Proposition
**ROI Promise**: 40% reduction in operational costs within 90 days
- Automate 70% of customer inquiries
- Increase sales team productivity by 50%
- Reduce response times from hours to minutes

### 4. Demo Script
**Opening**: "I'll show you how [Company] can automate their customer service and sales processes, potentially saving $X per month."

**Key Demo Points**:
1. Live chatbot handling customer inquiry
2. Sales pipeline automation in action  
3. Real-time analytics dashboard
4. ROI calculator with their numbers

### 5. Objection Handling
**"Too expensive"**: "At $299/month, if we save you just 20 hours of staff time, you're already profitable. Most clients see 3-5x ROI."

**"Too complex"**: "We handle the entire setup. You'll be live in 48 hours with our team managing everything."

**"Need to think about it"**: "I understand. What specific concerns can I address? Would a pilot program help?"

### 6. Closing
- Always propose 14-day free trial
- Create urgency with implementation timeline
- Schedule follow-up within 48 hours

## Success Metrics
- Target: $50K MRR within 60 days
- Conversion rate: 15% (industry average 2-5%)
- Average deal size: $299/month
