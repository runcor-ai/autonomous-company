# Lead Magnet Creator Template

## Overview
AI-powered system to create irresistible lead magnets that convert visitors into qualified leads.

## AI Prompt Template
```
Create a comprehensive lead magnet for [BUSINESS_TYPE] targeting [TARGET_AUDIENCE] struggling with [SPECIFIC_PAIN_POINT].

Lead Magnet Details:
Type: [CHECKLIST/TEMPLATE/GUIDE/TOOLKIT/WEBINAR/ASSESSMENT]
Title: Create 5 compelling title options
Subtitle: Supporting benefit statement
Landing page copy: Headline, benefits, social proof
Opt-in form: Email subject line suggestions

Content Structure:
1. Problem identification (what they're struggling with)
2. Solution framework (your methodology)
3. Step-by-step implementation
4. Common mistakes to avoid
5. Next steps/call-to-action

Bonus Materials:
- Worksheet/template
- Video walkthrough outline
- Email nurture sequence (3 emails)
- Social media promotion posts

Target outcome: [SPECIFIC_RESULT] in [TIMEFRAME]
Unique angle: [YOUR_DIFFERENTIATOR]
```

## High-Converting Lead Magnet Types

### Checklists (Best for beginners)
- Quick wins
- Easy to consume
- Immediate value
- Low commitment

### Templates (Best for DIYers)
- Copy-paste solutions
- Time-saving
- Professional results
- High perceived value

### Guides (Best for detail-oriented)
- Comprehensive information
- Authority building
- Educational content
- Long-term relationship

### Toolkits (Best for professionals)
- Multiple resources
- Complete solution
- High value perception
- Premium positioning

## Customization Variables
- [BUSINESS_TYPE]: Your industry/niche
- [TARGET_AUDIENCE]: Demographics and psychographics
- [SPECIFIC_PAIN_POINT]: Their biggest challenge
- [CHECKLIST/TEMPLATE/GUIDE/TOOLKIT]: Format choice
- [SPECIFIC_RESULT]: Measurable outcome
- [TIMEFRAME]: When they'll see results
- [YOUR_DIFFERENTIATOR]: What makes you unique

## Landing Page Conversion Elements
1. **Compelling Headline**: Clear benefit statement
2. **Bullet Points**: 3-5 specific benefits
3. **Social Proof**: Testimonials or statistics
4. **Urgency/Scarcity**: Limited time or quantity
5. **Risk Reversal**: Money-back guarantee
6. **Clear CTA**: Action-oriented button text

## Lead Nurture Sequence
**Email 1** (Immediate): Deliver lead magnet + set expectations
**Email 2** (Day 2): Case study of implementation success
**Email 3** (Day 5): Soft pitch for paid solution

## Success Metrics
- 25-40% conversion rate (visitor to lead)
- 60%+ email open rates in sequence
- 20%+ click-through rates
- 15% conversion from lead to customer (30 days)

## Implementation Timeline
- Day 1: Create lead magnet content
- Day 2: Design landing page
- Day 3: Set up email automation
- Day 4: Create promotion materials
- Day 5: Launch and drive traffic
